## Problem

E-commerce checkout had a 70% abandonment rate with users dropping off during shipping and payment steps.

## Solution

Implemented progressive disclosure, guest checkout option, and transparent pricing breakdown.

## Results

Reduced abandonment by 28% in A/B testing with improved user confidence and faster completion.
