## Context

The signup flow lost users at the verification step. The business needed lower abandonment without weakening legal requirements.

## Approach

- Heuristic audit and friction map
- Eight interviews with recent users
- Iteration on a high-fidelity prototype

## Outcome

Clearer copy, visible progress, and error recovery; prototype ready for A/B testing with engineering.
